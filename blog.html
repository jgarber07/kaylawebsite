My professional experiences have provided me the opportunity to work in residential treatment, substance abuse treatment, foster care, community mental health, and most recently, private practice. I am currently specializing in the treatment of trauma for both adolescents and adults. I have been trained in evidence based practices that have been shown to be highly effective for anxiety, depression, and trauma. You can learn more about those treatment models under the Counseling Services tab.

My mission is to work collaboratively and compassionately with you to address the areas you want to change in your life. I approach the therapy process with your strengths in mind, building upon them to give you the skills needed to harness strength and growth. In my office, you’ll find a comfortable, non-judgemental place to explore your goals and challenges. There is great power in sharing your story with a trusted person. I believe in that power, and I feel honored to be a part of that with each one of my clients.

When you’re ready, give me a call. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you!
